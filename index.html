Hair Stylist ✂

I am a highly motivated person with plenty of potential and I am seeking a position where I can further my knowledge in a fast paced environment where I can leverage my skills in. Currently I am finishing my last quarter at the University of California Riverside, and I will be receiving my Bachelors of Arts in June of 2020. I am a native Spanish speaker and I am currently working as a licensed cosmetologist. In this field I managed to provide excellent customer service and salon cleanliness while at the same time refining my skills in product inventory upkeep, receptionist tasks, maintain a steady flow of clientele, keep service outcomes in the art of cosmetology and barbery consistent, and juggle between chemical services and haircuts in a clean and professional manner. In this field the pace fluctuates from fast to steady, but for the most part a salon environment is a fast paced environment with clientele of all backgrounds. I have been a cosmetologist for the past 11+ years, and I have grown in this field with each passing year gaining more responsibilities such as opening and closing salons, closing salon registers and maintaining full stock of product by handling inventory.

My first job was at a clothing store where my position was a sales clerk. My customer service skills were at a developing stage due to the fact that I volunteered to work at a french restaurant where I familiarized myself with the different demographics and personalities of customers. As a sales clerk my duties were to maintain a clean and organized store while providing great customer service and closing the register. My skills were becoming refined as my responsibilities became bigger.

At my next job I became a licensed cosmetologist and started working in small salons where the pay was commission based. I started as someone that had no knowledge of how to do hair, let alone do my own hair. I started from zero and cultivated my own technique to the craft. This refining of technique took almost 6 years of my career to become perfected and flawless. While trying to perfect my craft, I also had to continue refining my customer service skills, and maintain salon cleanliness.

After perfecting my skills and learning much more along the way, I decided to focus on my education and earn my Bachelor of Arts in something that I love. My career as a cosmetologist helped me throughout my college life by helping with the financial strains that come with going to college. I managed to work my way through school, while at the same time continue my career as a cosmetologist. In June of 2020, I will finally achieve my goal of earning my degree while being honored for having the experience I have now in the beauty industry. I know after all is done I will have the confidence to branch out of the beauty industry and dive into something that will be my passion. Being a cosmetologist has prepared me with skills that I know will be essential to my career while helping me become the confident person I am today.
